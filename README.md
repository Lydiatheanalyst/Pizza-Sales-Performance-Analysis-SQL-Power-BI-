📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes transactional pizza sales data to evaluate overall business performance, customer behavior, product trends, and sales distribution.

The goal is to identify high-performing products, weak-performing products, peak sales periods, and areas for operational improvement.

📊 Core KPIs & Business Insights
1️⃣ Total Revenue

KPI:
$802,777.45

Insight:
The business generated over $800K in total sales within the dataset period.

Observation:
Revenue performance is strong, but total revenue alone does not reveal which products or time periods are driving growth.

Recommendation:
Focus on protecting top revenue drivers and investigate whether revenue growth is volume-driven or price-driven.

2️⃣ Total Orders

KPI:
21,231 orders

Insight:
This reflects total customer transactions.

Observation:
Order volume provides a clearer picture of demand compared to revenue alone.

Recommendation:
Track order growth month-over-month to monitor business expansion or decline.

3️⃣ Total Pizzas Sold

KPI:
48,994 pizzas

Insight:
High sales volume indicates strong product demand.

Observation:
Comparing this with total orders shows customers purchase multiple pizzas per order.

Recommendation:
Explore bundle offers to increase average order size further.

4️⃣ Average Order Value (AOV)

KPI:
$37.81

Insight:
On average, each customer spends nearly $38 per transaction.

Observation:
This indicates moderate spending behavior and room for upselling.

Recommendation:
Introduce premium add-ons or combo upgrades to increase AOV.

5️⃣ Average Pizzas per Order

KPI:
2 pizzas per order

Insight:
Most customers order more than one pizza.

Observation:
This suggests group consumption (families or shared meals).

Recommendation:
Create “2-Pizza Deals” or family-sized promotions to align with customer behavior.

📅 Time-Based Performance
6️⃣ Sales by Day of Week

Insight:

Friday has the highest sales.

Sunday has the lowest sales.

Observation:
Sales peak toward the end of the workweek.

Insight:

🍕 Product Performance Analysis
8️⃣ Revenue by Pizza Category

Insight:
Revenue distribution is balanced across Supreme, Classic, Chicken, and Veggie categories (each around 24–26%).

Observation:
No single category dominates revenue.

Recommendation:
Maintain balanced inventory across categories while testing promotions on slightly lower-performing segments.

9️⃣ Revenue by Pizza Size

Insight:
Large pizzas generate nearly 47% of total revenue.

Observation:
Customers strongly prefer large sizes.

Recommendation:

Prioritize large-size inventory.

Introduce price incentives to move medium sizes if needed.

Chicken-based pizzas dominate revenue performance.

By Revenue & Quantity:

Greek Pizza

Mediterranean Pizza

Observation:
These pizzas consistently underperform across revenue, order count, and quantity sold.

📈 Overall Business Observations

Revenue is healthy but concentrated in specific products.

Large pizzas are the strongest revenue driver.

Fridays are peak demand days.

A small group of pizzas consistently drive sales.

Several products consistently underperform and may be dragging margins.

🚀 Strategic Recommendations

Double down on top 5 revenue-generating pizzas.

Increase marketing during low-performing months.

Optimize inventory for large pizza sizes.

Introduce bundle pricing to increase average order value.

Reassess low-performing products for repositioning or removal.
